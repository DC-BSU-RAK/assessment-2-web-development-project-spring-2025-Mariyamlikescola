Nemesis

A relentless bio-organic weapon crafted by Umbrella, notorious for stalking its targets with terrifying determination.

Mr. X

An emotionless Tyrant sent to hunt down survivors and recover the G-virus; a powerful presence always watching.

Albert Wesker

A former Umbrella scientist turned antagonist with superhuman powers and a god complex.

Alcina Dimitrescu

A towering vampiric noblewoman and one of the Four Lords, feared for her cruelty and bloodlust.

Eveline

A bioweapon in the form of a young girl who can infect and mentally control others to create a family.

Jack Baker

A once-loving father turned violent and nearly immortal due to Eveline’s mold infection.

Marguerite Baker

Transformed by mold, she develops the ability to control insect swarms and stalk her prey with a twisted maternal instinct.

Mother Miranda

A fanatical biologist turned god-like entity, seeking to revive her daughter through the Megamycete.

Osmund Saddler

The cult leader of Los Illuminados who controls others using Las Plagas to achieve world domination.

Jack Krauser

A former U.S. agent turned mercenary, infected with Plaga and loyal to Saddler, known for his knife combat.
